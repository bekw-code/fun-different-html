HOW TO SEE THE 3D
Hold the image close to your face. Relax your eyes as if staring through the screen at something far away.
Slowly pull the screen back while keeping your eyes relaxed (diverged). You'll feel a "click" — the depth layer will pop forward.
Parallel method: Let your eyes drift apart until you see three panels — focus the middle one.
Cross-eyed method: Won't work with this type — use diverge/parallel only.
Brighter regions in the source image will appear closer. Takes 5–60 seconds. Patience is the technique.
